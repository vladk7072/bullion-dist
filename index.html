ABOUT US

We’re a team of passionate developers, designers, and business men.

We’re a team of passionate developers, designers, and businessmen involved for years in the Bullion market. Always looking for ways to evolve the market and make it more known and profitable for all, we decided to create the first Bullion actors database. The main goal of the platform is to facilitate collaboration and sharing of information in, between and within the actors in the bullion market.
